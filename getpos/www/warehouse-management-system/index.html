Inventory Management

GETPOS allows you to monitor quantities, set reorder points, and manage stock across multiple locations. This feature helps prevent stockouts and overstocking, ensuring optimal inventory levels at all times.

Warehouse Location Management

Assign specific areas for different types of products and track their exact locations. This feature simplifies the process of locating items, reducing the time spent searching and increasing overall warehouse efficiency.

Goods In Processes

Track incoming shipments, verify quantities, and update your inventory records accurately. This process helps maintain accurate stock levels and reduces discrepancies between received and ordered goods.

Mobile Functionality

Whether you're on the warehouse floor or offsite, you can manage your warehouse operations from your mobile device. This flexibility ensures that you can stay connected and in control, no matter where you are.

Shipping Management

Simplify the shipping process with integrated shipping management. Generate shipping labels, track shipments, and manage carriers all within GETPOS. This feature helps ensure that products are shipped promptly and reach customers in good condition.

With detailed pick lists and packing instructions, GETPOS ensures that orders are picked accurately and packed securely. This reduces errors and improves order accuracy, leading to happier customers.
